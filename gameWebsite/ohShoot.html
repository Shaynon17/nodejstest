Rules


First player to have all 10 bullets wins

Reloading removes a bullet from your holster and reloads you. You may only have one bullet loaded at a time.

You must have a bullet loaded to shoot successfully. After you shoot a bullet, it goes back to your holster. If you shoot someone who is reloading or trying to punch, you get a bullet from their holster. If both players shoot, the bullets cancel each other out.

Block only blocks bullets.

If you punch someone who is shooting, you lose a bullet from your holster. If you punch someone who is blocking, you take a bullet from their holster. If you punch someone who is reloading, you cancel their reload.
